Exploring a shared visual language for mixed-ability co-creation

Semio is an early research probe on visual symbols and AI for mixed-ability co-creation. It is intentionally incomplete — a starting point for critique by people with intellectual disabilities and their support networks, not a finished product. AI acts as a transparent interpreter to check meaning, not as a voice that speaks for anyone.

Explore the interactive demo
How the demo works
1

Compose with symbols

Tap symbols onto a shared canvas (drag also works on desktop).

2

Review the AI’s reading

The system proposes one possible meaning for what is on the canvas.

3

Confirm or correct

Say if it sounds right, try another reading, or leave it open.

Demo only: readings are pre-scripted (no live AI model). Runs in the browser with no data collected. Built for desktop and tablet, and open to critique.
